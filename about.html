About me

Hey there, I'm Reinaldo Cruz. I'm originally from Guaynabo, Puerto Rico where I grew up until leaving for college. Graduated in May 2015 from Georgia Tech (shoutout to ATL) with a B.S. in Computer Engineering. During my time there, I worked on a variety of projects that exposed me to the different tracks of Computer Science. My interests lie in machine learning, artificial intelligence, human-computer interaction, mobile and web-development just to name a few. I have experience writing reliable and eficient code but strive to strengthen my skills every day.

I'm currently living in Raleigh, NC working as a developer for IBM Cloud's MobileFirst Platform. I'm developing a NodeJS application and framework that allows users to create cross platform mobile applications wrapped in native code. This command-line interface interacts with a RESTful backend and deploys directly to an enterprise server.

When I'm not coding at work or on a side-project, you'll probably find my traveling or doing something crazy. I'm fascinated with visiting different parts of the world whether it be to see friends and family or own cultural interests. Other hobbies include snowboarding, playing soccer, running, weightlifting and casual gaming.
